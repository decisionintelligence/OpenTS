Rule:

For univariate forecasting algorithms, we consider 8,068 series, and the MSMAPE metric, thus having 8,068 unique evaluation settings. For each forecasting algorithm, we count the number of times that the algorithm achieves the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd accuarcy, denoated as x, y, and z. Finally, we calcualte the final score as score = 3 * x + 2 * y + 1 * z. The larger the final score, the higher the ranking.
