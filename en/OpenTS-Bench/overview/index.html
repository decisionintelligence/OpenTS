OpenTS-Bench

OpenTS-Bench is a comprehensive and fair benchmarking of time series analytics, mainly including foreacsting and anaomly detection. OpenTS-Bench inlucdes Time series Forecasting Benchmark (TFB), Time series Anomaly detection Benchmark (TAB), and Time Series Foundation Model Benchmark (TSFM-Bench). OpenTS provides comprehensive time series datasets, rich time series analtycis algorithms, and a unified evaluation pipeline with various strategies and metrics.

OpenTS-Bench empowers you with three key capabilities：benchmark your own algorithm, test your custom dataset, and track algorithm rankings. Please click here to get started.

Comprehensive Datasets

OpenTS offers comprehensive time series benchmark datasets with diverse characteristics from multiple domains and complex settings.

Rich Algorithms

OpenTS covers a diverse range of methods, including statistical learning, machine learning, deep learning methods, and time series foundation models.

Unified Pipeline

OpenTS offers a unified pipeline for evaluating time series forecasting and anomaly detection methods under a variety of experiment configuration settings.
