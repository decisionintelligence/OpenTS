OpenTS-BM

OpenTS-BM is a comprehensive and fair benchmarking of time series analytics, mainly including foreacsting and anaomly detection. OpenTS-BM inlucdes Time series Forecasting Benchmark (TFB), Time series Anomaly detection Benchmark (TAB), and Time Series Foundation Model Benchmark (TSFM-Bench). OpenTS provides comprehensive time series datasets, rich time series analtycis algorithms, and a unified evaluation pipeline with various strategies and metrics.

Comprehensive Datasets

OpenTS offers comprehensive time series benchmark datasets with diverse characteristics from multiple domains and complex settings.

Rich Algorithms

OpenTS covers a diverse range of methods, including statistical learning, machine learning, deep learning methods, and time series foundation models.

Unified Pipeline

OpenTS offers a unified pipeline for evaluating time series forecasting and anomaly detection methods under a variety of experiment configuration settings.
